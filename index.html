Grizzley Bears

Bears are large mammals that eat mostly meat and fish. Many bears also eat plants, bulbs, and insects.
The polar bear eats mostly meat and is classified as carnivores, while the panda feeds almost entirely on bamboo and is classifified as a herbivore. The six other species of bear eat both plants and animals and are classified as omnivorous.
